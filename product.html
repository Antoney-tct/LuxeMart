Shipping Information

We offer two shipping options for all orders within Kenya:

  • Standard Shipping (3–5 business days) — Free on orders over KSh 5,000. KSh 350 for orders below.
  • Express Shipping (1–2 business days) — KSh 650 flat rate. Available for select areas.

Return Policy

We offer a 30-day return policy for all items in their original condition with tags attached. To initiate a return, contact our support team at support@luxemart.co.ke with your order number.

Damaged Items

If your item arrives damaged, email us a photo within 48 hours of delivery and we'll arrange a replacement or full refund immediately.
